About SmartLife

SmartLife Innovations Solutions Limited is an education technology company focused on
leveraging technology to enhance learning and teach skills that are 21st century compliant and
easily marketable.

According to UNESCO over 52 million children and teenagers are out of school in Africa
alone, unfortunately, the outbreak of the Covid-19 pandemic has worsened the situation and
made online learning in and out of School for students and youths Imperative. The face of
Education is now changing rapidly in response to the general demand for personalized
learning. We are working to "disrupt" education by bringing the classroom online and aligning
market demands with educational investments.
